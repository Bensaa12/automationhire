Maintenance Tips

🔥 Boiler — service annually. Most failures happen Oct–Dec.

🏚️ Roof — inspect gutters spring & autumn. Replace felt every 15–20 yrs.

💧 Plumbing — know where your stopcock is. Check for leaks annually.

⚡ Electrics — EICR certificate every 10 years for owner-occupiers.

🌿 Budget — a common rule: set aside 1% of property value per year for maintenance.
