How to get the most from your call

1

Think about your biggest time drain

What does your team spend the most time on manually? Invoicing? Follow-ups? Customer support? Come with one or two examples.

2

List the tools you use

Note down your current software stack — CRM, email, e-commerce platform, etc. This helps us suggest the right integrations.

3

Know your rough budget range

You don't need a fixed number — just a sense of whether you're looking for a quick win or a bigger transformation project.

4

Have questions ready

Write down anything you're curious about — AI tools, timelines, what's realistic for your industry. No question is too basic.
