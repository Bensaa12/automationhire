About FlowCraft Agency

We're a London-based AI automation agency helping businesses eliminate repetitive work and scale operations without hiring more staff. Founded in 2019, we've delivered 120+ automation projects across the UK and Europe, saving our clients an average of 40+ hours per week.

Our team of 8 automation engineers specialises in Make.com (formerly Integromat), n8n, and custom AI agents powered by GPT-4 and LangChain. We work with SMEs, scale-ups, and enterprise clients across e-commerce, SaaS, real estate, and professional services.

Whether you need a simple 3-step Zapier flow or a complex multi-agent AI pipeline, we scope, build, test, and hand over fully documented automations — with ongoing support included.
