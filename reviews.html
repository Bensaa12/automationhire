🛡 How AutomationHire verifies reviews

We can't accept unverifiable reviews — buyers wouldn't trust them and Google's policies on review schema are strict. Every rating on this page meets all four of the following:

  • Engagement-gated. Only clients with a paid engagement booked through our quote-matching flow can leave a review. The engagement reference and invoice are checked before publication.
  • First-party only. Reviews are submitted directly to AutomationHire by the verified buyer. We do not scrape third-party sites or repurpose social media posts.
  • Non-incentivised. Providers cannot offer discounts, kickbacks, or follow-up work in exchange for a review. Doing so is grounds for removal from the directory.
  • Provider response only — never edit. A provider can publicly respond to any review on their profile, but cannot edit, hide, or pay to remove it. Reviews are removed only for breach of guidelines (defamation, off-topic, personal data, failed verification).

The verification trail (engagement reference, reviewer email, publish date) is retained internally and is available to UK regulators on request.

FAQ

Reviews · Frequently Asked Questions

How does AutomationHire verify reviews?

Every rating comes from a UK business that booked a paid engagement with a provider through AutomationHire's quote-matching flow. We verify the engagement happened (invoice or contract reference) before the rating is published, and we publish only first-party, non-incentivised reviews.

What does the 4.8/5 average rating include?

The 4.8/5 figure is the unweighted mean across 222 individual client ratings collected on the profiles of our six featured verified providers. The breakdown by provider is published on this page so the number is independently checkable.

Can providers pay to remove negative reviews?

No. AutomationHire never removes a verified review at a provider's request. Providers may publicly respond to any review on their profile. Reviews are only removed if they breach our guidelines (defamation, off-topic, contains personal data, or fails verification).

How do I leave a review for a provider I hired?

Once your engagement is marked complete in the dashboard you'll receive a one-time review link by email. You can rate 1-5 stars across delivery, communication, and outcome, plus leave a written review. Reviews are published within 48 hours after verification.

Are these reviews independently audited?

Reviews are not currently audited by a third party. The verification trail (engagement reference, reviewer email, publish date) is retained internally and is available to UK regulators on request. We're evaluating Trustpilot integration for 2026.

Why is the rating so high — are you filtering out bad reviews?

No. The rating is high because we vet providers before they're listed (60% of applications are rejected on first review) and because clients self-select their match through our quote flow, which improves fit. Distribution: 71% of ratings are 5-star, 20% are 4-star, 7% are 3-star, 2% are 1-2 star.

Can I see reviews for a specific provider before I hire them?

Yes — every provider profile shows their full review history, individual star ratings across delivery/communication/outcome, written client feedback, and the provider's public responses. You can browse providers on the directory and filter by minimum rating.
