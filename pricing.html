Frequently Asked Questions

Is the free plan really free forever? ▾
Yes — the Free plan is genuinely free with no time limit and no credit card required. You get a basic listing that appears in our directory. You can upgrade anytime when you want more visibility and features.
Do you take commission on projects? ▾
No. We never take commission on deals between you and your clients. Your subscription is a flat fee for directory visibility and lead access. What you earn is 100% yours.
What counts as an "inbound lead"? ▾
A lead is when a business submits a quote request directly to your profile, or when our matching system sends your profile to a buyer. You receive the lead's contact details and project brief directly.
How does the Verified badge work? ▾
The Verified badge is included in Growth and Pro plans. Our team manually reviews your profile, checks your portfolio, certifications, and company details. Most verifications complete within 48 hours of your first billing.
Can I cancel anytime? ▾
Yes, cancel anytime from your dashboard. You keep your paid features until the end of your billing period, then drop to the Free plan. No exit fees, no questions asked.
